# Introduction to Control D

<Info>
Control D is a powerful DNS filtering and security platform that gives you control over your internet experience.
</Info>

## Overview

Control D provides a comprehensive solution for managing and securing your internet access through DNS filtering. With Control D, you can:

- Block unwanted content and ads
- Protect against malware and phishing
- Control access to specific websites and services
- Monitor and analyze your network activity
- Customize filtering rules to your needs
