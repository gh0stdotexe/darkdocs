## Getting Help

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Check our [Status Page](/control-d/web-dashboard/status) for any known service issues
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Collect relevant logs and diagnostic information
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Contact our support team with:
    - Description of the issue
    - Steps to reproduce
    - Relevant logs and diagnostics
    - Platform and version information
  </Step>
</Steps>
