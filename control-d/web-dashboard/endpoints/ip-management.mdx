<header id="content-head"><div class="row clearfix"><div class="col-xs-9"># IP Management

<div class="excerpt"><div class="rm-Markdown markdown-body" data-testid="RDMD"><p>View IPs that queried a device, or are authorized to use it.</p></div></div></div><div class="col-xs-3">[<i aria-hidden="true" class="icon icon-edit"></i> Suggest Edits](/edit/ip-management)</div></div></header><div class="grid-container-fluid" id="content-container"><section class="content-body grid-75"><div dehydrated="# <div class=&quot;heading-anchor anchor waypoint&quot; id=&quot;what-is-ip-management&quot;></div><div class=&quot;heading-text&quot;><div id=&quot;section-what-is-ip-management&quot; class=&quot;heading-anchor_backwardsCompatibility&quot;></div>What is IP Management</div><Link aria-label=&quot;Skip link to What is IP Management&quot; class=&quot;heading-anchor-icon fa fa-anchor&quot; href=&quot;#what-is-ip-management&quot;></Link>

<p>This feature is optional depending on what types of Devices you use, what physical gadgets you use them, and what your privacy criteria is. This web control panel section allows you to view IPs that have interacted with your Device specific DNS resolvers, or restrict what IPs that are allowed to communicate with them. </p>
## <div class=&quot;heading-anchor anchor waypoint&quot; id=&quot;how-to-use&quot;></div><div class=&quot;heading-text&quot;><div id=&quot;section-how-to-use&quot; class=&quot;heading-anchor_backwardsCompatibility&quot;></div>How to Use</div><Link aria-label=&quot;Skip link to How to Use&quot; class=&quot;heading-anchor-icon fa fa-anchor&quot; href=&quot;#how-to-use&quot;></Link>

<p>From the Manage Profiles &amp; Devices section, select &quot;<Link href=&quot;https://controld.com/dashboard/ips&quot; target=&quot;&quot; title=&quot;&quot;>View IPs</Link>&quot; from the drop down menu. </p>
<span aria-label=&quot;884&quot; class=&quot;img lightbox closed&quot; role=&quot;button&quot; tabindex=&quot;0&quot;><span class=&quot;lightbox-inner&quot;><Image alt=&quot;884&quot; loading=&quot;lazy&quot; src=&quot;https://files.readme.io/8ff9d91-view_ips.JPG&quot; title=&quot;view_ips.JPG&quot; align=&quot;center&quot; class=&quot;&quot; width=&quot;80%&quot; caption=&quot;&quot; height=&quot;auto&quot;/></span></span>
<p>Depending on your Device Settings, this will either show you nothing, or a list of IPs that have interacted or are allowed to interact with your Device specific resolver. </p>
### <div class=&quot;heading-anchor anchor waypoint&quot; id=&quot;secure-dns-only&quot;></div><div class=&quot;heading-text&quot;><div id=&quot;section-secure-dns-only&quot; class=&quot;heading-anchor_backwardsCompatibility&quot;></div>Secure DNS Only</div><Link aria-label=&quot;Skip link to Secure DNS Only&quot; class=&quot;heading-anchor-icon fa fa-anchor&quot; href=&quot;#secure-dns-only&quot;></Link>

<p>If your Device uses Secure DNS protocols, you can Disable the <Link href=&quot;/docs/auto-authorize-ip&quot; target=&quot;&quot; title=&quot;&quot;>Auto Authorize IP</Link> feature, which will prevent source IPs from being logged on that page. </p>
### <div class=&quot;heading-anchor anchor waypoint&quot; id=&quot;legacy-resolvers&quot;></div><div class=&quot;heading-text&quot;><div id=&quot;section-legacy-resolvers&quot; class=&quot;heading-anchor_backwardsCompatibility&quot;></div>Legacy Resolvers</div><Link aria-label=&quot;Skip link to Legacy Resolvers&quot; class=&quot;heading-anchor-icon fa fa-anchor&quot; href=&quot;#legacy-resolvers&quot;></Link>

<p>If your Device requires <Link href=&quot;/docs/legacy-resolver&quot; target=&quot;&quot; title=&quot;&quot;>Legacy DNS Resolvers</Link>, the IP logging feature cannot be disabled, as the meaning of it changes. It now functions as a list of IPs that are allowed to interact with your Legacy DNS resolver. </p>
